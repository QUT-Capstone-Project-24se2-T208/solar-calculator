How SEMS Works

The TBB Power Raython system is designed for seamless automatic switching between various power sources like grid, solar, battery, and a generator.

Prioritized Power Supply

The system operates based on user-configurable priority settings, allowing you to define which power source should be utilized first.

Intelligent Monitoring

SEMS constantly monitors the availability and status of each power source, including solar energy generation, battery state, grid stability, and generator status.

Automatic Transfer Switch

The integrated high-speed automatic transfer switch physically changes the power source to your appliances with zero interruption.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need solar panels to use the SEMS?

No, that's one of the key advantages of our system. The SEMS can work with or without solar panels, managing power from grid, generator, and batteries. Solar panels can be added to the system at any time.

How quickly does the system switch between power sources?

The switching occurs within milliseconds, fast enough to prevent any interruption to sensitive appliances like computers. Most users don't even notice when the system changes from one source to another.

Is the NOVA platform really free?

Yes, the NOVA cloud-based monitoring platform is included at no additional cost with your SEMS purchase. This includes both the web interface and mobile applications.

What kind of warranty comes with the system?

TE PNG provides a comprehensive 5-10 year warranty (depending on components) that's backed by both ourselves and our manufacturers, ensuring you have complete peace of mind.
